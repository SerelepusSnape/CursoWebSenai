The Great Emu War

When the animal fights back!

The full history:

It started in Campion, Australia, in November 2nd in the year of 1932 ; the emus were attacking australian farms and eating the vegetables.

The australian economy was getting affected! So the australians attack them!

But sadly and unfortunate, they lost the "war". It only ended in December 10 of the same year, because the emus emigrated to another place.

The results were:

  1. 100 emu deaths
  2. 0 human deaths
